🐍 Snake 3-Putt Game▼
Whoever holds the 3-putt snake after your 3rd, 6th, 9th, 12th, 15th and 18th holes pays each other participant the selected wager. The Snake resets after each settlement hole.

2v2 Nassau Games (High-Low)▼
Select as many pairings as you wish — Nassau Golf will score all of them. Tap a pairing to add it, then set the bet amount.
1v1 Nassau Games▼
Select as many matchups as you wish — Nassau Golf will score all of them. Tap a matchup to add it, then set the bet amount.
🎯 6-Point Game▼
Pick any 3 players for a separate 6-Point game (lowest player dropped to 0, net, no presses — 6 points up for grabs every hole).
🏆 Stableford▼
Individual points game. Full handicap applied. Highest points total wins — loser pays winner the points difference × bet.
⚔️ Match Cards▶
Match cards with up to 2 players from other groups. They're not scored during your round — at round end a separate ⚔️ Match Cards round is saved (your scores prefilled). Enter their scores later from History. You play a 1v1 Nassau vs each.
